Rail Namnoc: Con-man extrordinaire

About Rail

Rail is a dragonborn artificer who uses his smarts, cunning and talents to scam whoever he can to make money or obtain magical items

Rail's Characteristics

  1. He is a black dragonborn
  2. He has bright green eyes
  3. His horns protrude out from above his ears downwards and towards hiw snout
  4. He is smaller than most dragonborns

Rail's Equipment

Rail has accumulated much throughout his life as he tends to be drawn towards magic items and becoming more powerful

  • Wand of Magic Missiles
  • Dragonguard Armor
  • Goggles of Night
  • Bag of Holding
